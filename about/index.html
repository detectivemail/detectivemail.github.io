"I love writing detective stories.
And I call myself a Sherlockian."

The name Sherlockian is actually something I found somewhere else. It means like, the fan base of Sherlock Holmes. So I thought, the works done by me, could somewhat be done by any Sherlockian. But then, if we are all Sherlockians, what happens to my name? Easy, just name myself The Sherlockian & the problem is solved!

Hi! I’m the author of Detectivemail & my name is Md. Mazidul Haque Farabi AKA The Sherlockian. I am still a college student from Bangladesh. Currently into managing Thynkzone . I built this website literally being inspired from the hit series Sherlock. As I’m really a fan of Benedict and Freeman, you may also think our detectives Alex and Miles are sort of like them!

The site’s functionality is super easy! You can either choose read from Case 01, or even pick any episode or chapter of your choosing. The name may somewhat suggest the way the story goes, but the short description below, will make it fully clear to you. And after you read it, just click on the answer you think is correct, and reveal what happens, meaning the actual mystery.
